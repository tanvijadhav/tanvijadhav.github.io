About Me

I am a Systems Engineer at Kumu Networks where I design, implement and test algorithms for Self Intereference Cancellation (SLIC) to enable Full Duplex Communication.

My research interests are Machine Learning Theory, High Dimensional Statistics, Information Theory, and Wireless Communication Systems.

Previously, I completed my B.E.(Hons.) in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from BITS Pilani, K. K. Birla Goa Campus, India in 2017. I then came to the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, where I completed my Master of Science in Electrical and Electronics Engineering with communications systems as my major in May 2019. While at Michigan, I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. S. Sandeep Pradhan.
